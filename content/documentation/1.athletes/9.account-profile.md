Your **Profile** settings tell Coach Watts who you are as an athlete. Accurate profile data improves zone calculations, fueling targets, and AI plan generation. This page is an overview; use the [Settings guides](/documentation/athletes/settings) for complete field-level and workflow documentation.

Open **Profile → Settings** from the sidebar.

## Basic settings

Configure your identity and display preferences:

- **Name** — shown on shared workouts and coach views
- **Timezone** — affects how dates and daily metrics are grouped
- **Units** — metric (kg, km) or imperial (lb, miles)
- **Date of birth & sex** — used in BMR and metabolic calculations

## Sport settings

Add one or more **sport profiles** (Cycling, Running, Triathlon, etc.). Each profile stores:

- **Threshold values** — FTP (cycling), threshold pace (running), LTHR
- **Zone definitions** — power, heart rate, or pace zones
- **Activity type mapping** — which synced activities use this profile

::alert{type="warning"}
Avoid overlapping sport profiles that match the same activity types. Overlaps can cause unexpected zone choices during workout generation.
::

### Auto-detect thresholds

Use **Auto-detect** to estimate FTP or threshold pace from your recent workout history. Review the suggested values before saving — they are starting points, not gospel.

## Measurements

Track body metrics used across nutrition and performance analysis:

- **Weight** — updates fueling calculations (g/kg carb targets)
- **Body composition** — optional, synced from Withings or entered manually
- **VO2 Max** — if available from your devices

Weight changes propagate to nutrition targets on the next calculation.

## Training availability

Define when you can train. The AI uses this when generating plans:

- **Days of the week** you are available
- **Time slots** per day (morning, lunch, evening)
- **Maximum sessions** per day

Accurate availability prevents the coach from scheduling workouts when you cannot complete them.

## Nutrition settings

Fine-tune how fueling targets are calculated:

- **Fueling sensitivity** — scale carb targets up or down (0.5×–1.5×)
- **Training phase preset** — Base, Build, or Race metabolic emphasis
- **Goal profile** — lose, maintain, or gain weight
- **Fuel state triggers** — customize intensity thresholds for Eco, Steady, and Performance states

See [Nutrition & Fueling Logic](/documentation/athletes/fueling-logic) for how these settings affect your daily targets.

## Public presence

Control what others see on your public athlete profile:

- Profile visibility and slug
- Shared stats and achievements
- Social links

## Communication preferences

Manage email notifications:

- Training guidance emails
- Weekly report delivery
- Product updates

## AI Coach settings

Separate from Profile, go to **Settings → AI Coach** to configure:

- Coach personality and communication style
- Language preferences
- Feature toggles for automated recommendations

## Goals

Set athletic goals at **Profile → Goals** (sidebar). Goals anchor training plan generation:

- **Body composition** — target weight or body fat
- **Event preparation** — race date, distance, terrain
- **Performance** — FTP target, pace improvement
- **Consistency** — training frequency targets

See [Goals & Events](/documentation/athletes/goals-events) for details.
