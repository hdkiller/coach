title: Activity Calendar & Workout Management
description: Use calendar and list views, move sessions, match planned workouts, manage duplicates, and clean up activity history.
---

The **Activities** page is the main operational view for completed workouts, planned sessions, wellness entries, nutrition, rest days, and events.

## Calendar and list views

Use the view buttons in the header to switch between:

- **Calendar** — best for planning, matching, rescheduling, and weekly context
- **List** — best for scanning a longer activity history

Use the date controls to move through time. Filters and calendar settings help reduce visual noise when you only need a particular sport or record type.

## Open an activity

Select a completed workout to open its detail page. Select a planned workout to inspect its structure, targets, and completion state. Wellness and nutrition entries open their respective daily detail views.

## Reschedule a planned workout

Drag a planned workout to another date and confirm the move. Rescheduling changes the calendar assignment; it does not create a second workout.

If a connected planning platform also owns the workout, allow synchronization to finish before making another change.

## Match a completed workout to a plan

Coach Watts attempts to match imported activities to planned sessions automatically. To correct or create a match manually:

1. Drag the completed workout onto the planned workout, or open the page menu and select **Link Workouts**.
2. Review the planned and completed pair.
3. Confirm the link.

The planned workout becomes completed and its adherence analysis can use the recorded activity. A completed workout can only have one planned-workout link.

To remove a bad match, open the completed workout and select **Unlink** in the plan-adherence section.

## Merge duplicate completed workouts

Drag the secondary completed workout onto the completed workout you want to keep. The destination remains primary and the dragged workout becomes a linked duplicate.

For a full history scan and data-quality review, use **Activities → Deduplicate** instead. See [Duplicate Workouts & Versions](/documentation/athletes/duplicate-workouts).

## Add workouts from the library

Open the workout drawer in calendar view to browse reusable templates. Drag or schedule a template onto a date, then review the generated planned workout. You can also save a useful completed workout to your library for future reuse.

## Delete activities in bulk

Open **Activities → Manage** to select and delete multiple workouts. Deletion is different from deduplication:

- Deduplication retains every source record and links secondary versions.
- Deletion removes the selected records.

Use [Data Management](/documentation/athletes/data-management) when you need to review individual records across workouts, wellness, events, and nutrition.

## Mobile drag actions

On smaller screens, start the drag action from the workout card, move to the target day or workout, and release on the highlighted destination. If precise drag-and-drop is difficult, use **Link Workouts** or open the workout directly for the equivalent action.

## Common mistakes

- Dropping a planned workout onto another planned workout does not merge completed activities.
- Only completed-to-completed drops create duplicate-version links.
- Linking a completed workout to a planned workout is not deduplication.
- Removing an integration does not automatically remove activities already imported from it.
