Welcome to Coach Watts! This guide will help you get your account set up and your first activities synced.

## Steps

1. **Create an account**: Sign up with your email.
2. **Connect a service**: Go to Settings > Apps and connect Strava, Garmin, or Oura.
3. **Wait for ingestion**: We'll start pulling your data immediately.
4. **Chat with your coach**: Ask questions about your training.
