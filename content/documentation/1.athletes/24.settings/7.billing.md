---
title: Billing & Entitlements
description: Understand subscription status, plan changes, feature access, AI quotas, and payment recovery.
---

Open **Settings → Billing**. Billing is shown when subscription payments are configured for the deployment.

## Review the current plan

The page shows the active tier, billing status, renewal information, and included entitlements. Entitlements determine feature access; AI quotas determine how much of an entitled AI operation remains available.

## Upgrade or change plan

Select the desired plan and complete checkout. After returning to Coach Watts, allow verification to finish before starting another checkout or refreshing repeatedly.

For an existing paid subscription, use the subscription-management action to update payment information, change the plan, or cancel where supported.

## Past-due subscriptions

A past-due banner means payment could not be completed. Open the billing portal, update the payment method, and return to Coach Watts. Access may remain restricted until the provider confirms the subscription status.

## AI quotas

Open **Settings → AI Coach** for quota details and usage history. Some quotas reset at midnight; rolling quotas recover gradually. Upgrading can increase limits but does not repair a failed source-data import.

## Checkout canceled or unavailable

Canceling checkout leaves the existing subscription unchanged. If billing is unavailable, retry later or use the Help Center rather than creating multiple checkout sessions.
