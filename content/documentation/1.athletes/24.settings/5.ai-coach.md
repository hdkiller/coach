---
title: AI Coach Settings
description: Configure coach personality, voice, data access, automatic analysis, tool approval, and usage limits.
---

Open **Settings → AI Coach**.

## Personality and communication

Configure the coach name, communication tone, and voice persona. These settings affect presentation and response style, not the underlying workout measurements.

**Conversational Engagement** allows proactive follow-up questions. Disable it when you prefer short, single-response interactions.

## Voice

Open **Configure Voice** to choose the read-aloud voice and automatic playback behavior. Preview a voice before saving. Browser audio permissions and device output still control whether sound can play.

## Data and notes

- **Enable Nutrition Analysis** permits the coach to use nutrition data in feedback.
- **Update Workout Notes** permits AI workout summaries to be captured as notes.

Disable a data category when you do not want it included in AI coaching context.

## Identity and context

Use nickname, athlete bio, and **About Me** to describe experience, injuries, limitations, goals, or preferences. Keep this current and avoid conflicting instructions.

This context guides personalization but does not replace structured thresholds, availability, medical advice, or plan settings.

## Automation

Depending on subscription tier, automation can include:

- Automatic readiness analysis
- Automatic workout analysis
- Automatic nutrition analysis
- Thoughtful/deep analysis
- Proactive coaching
- Approval before AI tools change data

Keep tool approval enabled when you want to review mutations before they occur. Some features require Supporter or Pro access.

## Usage, quotas, and history

The page displays usage charts, operation history, and current quotas. Calendar-day quotas reset at midnight; rolling quotas refill as older usage leaves the displayed window.

If an operation is unavailable, check both the remaining quota and plan entitlement before troubleshooting the underlying data.
