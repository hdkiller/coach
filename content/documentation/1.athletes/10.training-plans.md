Coach Watts builds **dynamic, goal-driven training plans** that adapt when life gets in the way. Unlike static PDF plans, your schedule updates based on recovery, missed sessions, and performance trends.

## How plans work

Training plans follow a periodization hierarchy:

| Level          | Name            | Example                         |
| -------------- | --------------- | ------------------------------- |
| **Macrocycle** | Full plan       | 12-week build to a century ride |
| **Mesocycle**  | Training block  | 4-week sweet spot block         |
| **Microcycle** | Training week   | Week 3: 320 TSS target          |
| **Session**    | Planned workout | Tuesday: 60min tempo intervals  |

Open **Training Plan** from the sidebar to view your active plan.

## Creating a plan

Use the **Plan Wizard** to generate a new plan:

1. **Select or create a goal** — race, fitness milestone, or event date
2. **Profile the event** — distance, elevation, terrain, priority
3. **Set availability** — training days and time slots (from [Profile settings](/documentation/athletes/account-profile))
4. **Choose strategy** — linear, polarized, or pyramidal intensity distribution
5. **Set volume preference** — low, moderate, or high weekly hours
6. **Preview block structure** — review mesocycles before activating

::alert{type="info"}
Your availability and threshold values directly affect plan quality. Complete [Account & Profile Setup](/documentation/athletes/account-profile) before generating a plan.
::

## Plan dashboard

The active plan view shows:

- **Timeline** — horizontal progression through training blocks
- **Week overview** — TSS and volume targets for the selected week
- **Workout list** — planned sessions with completion status
- **Compliance** — how closely completed workouts match the plan

![A recommended training plan showing the week's recovery sessions, availability, duration, TSS, intensity, and schedule actions](/media/docs/training-plan-overview.jpg)

The weekly table makes it easy to review the load and intent of every session before adding it to your schedule.

## Planned vs. completed workouts

When you finish a synced activity, Coach Watts attempts to **match** it to a planned workout. Unmatched sessions appear in the workout matcher for manual linking.

Matching affects:

- Plan compliance metrics
- Block progression decisions
- AI adaptation recommendations

## Adapting your plan

Life happens. Use the **Adaptation Wizard** or ask Chat to adjust:

- **Push forward** — shift remaining workouts after a missed week
- **Recalculate** — reduce intensity based on fatigue signals
- **Mark sick** — insert rest days and adjust the return-to-training ramp
- **Swap sessions** — exchange today's workout for something easier

Example Chat prompts:

- _"I missed three days — can we push my plan forward?"_
- _"I'm sick today. Adjust the rest of this week."_
- _"Make this block 10% easier based on my recent HRV."_

## Sharing plans

Share your plan with a coach or training partner:

1. Open **Training Plan**
2. Click **Share**
3. Copy the link or send it directly

Connected coaches can review your schedule and manage supported planned-workout actions through permission-checked [coach views](/documentation/coaches/acting-as-athlete) and the [coaching calendar](/documentation/coaches/coaching-calendar).

## Library plans

Browse pre-built templates at **Library → Plans**. Coaches can assign library plans to athletes from the [Coach Library](/documentation/coaches/coach-library) guide.
