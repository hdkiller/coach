Coach Watts provides a suite of tools designed to optimize your endurance training through artificial intelligence and deep data integration.

## AI Coaching

Our core engine analyzes your historical data to provide real-time feedback and session adjustments.

### Personalized Feedback

After every workout, our AI evaluates your performance relative to your target zones.

### Dynamic Adjustments

If you miss a session or overreach, the coach automatically updates your training plan.

## Training Plans

Build custom training blocks for Cycling, Running, or Triathlon.

### Block Periodization

We use advanced physiological models to structure your base, build, and peak phases.

### Event Integration

Sync your race calendar from Intervals.icu or Strava to anchor your plan.

## Nutrition Tracking

Integrated fueling advice based on your planned training intensity.

## Data Integrations

We support syncing from the most popular platforms:

- Strava
- Garmin Connect
- Oura
- Intervals.icu
- Whoop
- Yazio
