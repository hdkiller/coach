## AI Coaching

Our core engine analyzes your historical data to provide real-time feedback and session adjustments.

- **Personalized feedback** — after every workout, the AI evaluates performance relative to your target zones
- **Dynamic adjustments** — if you miss a session or overreach, the coach updates your training plan
- **Daily recommendations** — proceed, modify, or rest based on recovery and load
- **Background execution** — complex plan generation runs asynchronously with notifications

## Training Plans

Build goal-driven training blocks for cycling, running, or triathlon.

- **Block periodization** — base, build, peak, and taper phases structured around your event
- **Plan wizard** — set goals, availability, and strategy before generating
- **Adaptation** — push forward, recalculate, or mark sick when life intervenes
- **Workout matching** — link completed activities to planned sessions

## Data Integrations

Connect your training ecosystem — see the full [Integrations](/documentation/athletes/integrations) guide.

**Activity sources:** Strava, Garmin, Intervals.icu, Wahoo, ROUVY (via Strava/Garmin)

**Wellness:** Oura, WHOOP, Garmin, Fitbit, Polar, Withings

**Nutrition:** Yazio

**Messaging:** Telegram

For provider preferences, future-import behavior, and source conflicts, see [Connected Apps & Sync Settings](/documentation/athletes/settings/apps-sync). For automatic and reviewed matching, see [Duplicate Workouts & Versions](/documentation/athletes/duplicate-workouts).

## Activity and data management

- Calendar and list views for completed and planned workouts
- Drag-and-drop rescheduling, planned/completed matching, and manual merges
- Reviewed duplicate scanning with primary-version selection
- Workout versions that can be promoted or unlinked
- Individual and category-wide data cleanup

See [Activity Calendar & Workout Management](/documentation/athletes/activity-management) and [Data Management](/documentation/athletes/data-management).

## Libraries and advanced analytics

Create reusable workouts, strength exercises, plans, and custom charts. Workout Explorer and Workout Comparison support deeper investigation of intervals, metrics, and streams.

See [Workout, Exercise & Plan Libraries](/documentation/athletes/library) and [Analytics Explorer & Workout Comparison](/documentation/athletes/analytics-tools).

## Metrics & scoring

Every workout, nutrition day, and training period receives AI-generated **1–10 scores** with explanations. See [Metrics & Scoring](/documentation/athletes/metrics-scoring) and the [Glossary](/documentation/help/glossary).
