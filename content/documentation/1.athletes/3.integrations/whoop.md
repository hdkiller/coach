[Whoop](https://whoop.com) provides 24/7 physiological monitoring, tracking your Strain, Recovery, and Sleep. Coach Watts uses this data to adjust your training intensity based on your body's recovery.

## Setup Guide

1. Go to **Settings > Apps** on the Dashboard.
2. Click **Connect with Whoop** and authorize Coach Watts.

## What Syncs?

- **Strain**: Overall physical load from your day.
- **Recovery**: Daily recovery score (0-100%).
- **Sleep**: Performance and sleep stages.
- **HRV**: Heart Rate Variability and Resting Heart Rate.

::alert{type="info"}
Tip: Whoop data is updated automatically throughout the day as it syncs with the Whoop cloud.
::
