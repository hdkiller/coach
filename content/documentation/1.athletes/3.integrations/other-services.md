title: Other Services (Fitbit, Polar, Hevy)
description: Connect Fitbit, Polar, or Hevy for multi-source training and strength data.
---

Coach Watts supports several additional integrations to ensure we capture all your training data:

## Fitbit

Connect your **Fitbit** for activity tracking, daily steps, and basic sleep analysis.

## Polar

Sync your **Polar Flow** activities and training load directly to the Coach Watts dashboard.

## Hevy

If you incorporate strength training, **Hevy** allows you to sync your weightlifting sessions and set PRs.

::alert{type="info"}
Note: These services can be connected from the same **Settings > Apps** menu on your Dashboard.
::
