Coach Watts produces **retrospective AI reports** that analyze your training over longer periods. Where daily recommendations look forward, reports look back — identifying patterns, progress, and areas for improvement.

## Report types

| Type        | Scope        | Content                                                        |
| ----------- | ------------ | -------------------------------------------------------------- |
| **Weekly**  | Last 7 days  | Training load, key sessions, recovery trends, next-week focus  |
| **Monthly** | Last 30 days | Fitness progression, consistency, goal alignment, block review |

Reports are generated as structured markdown with scores across training load, recovery, progress, and consistency.

## Viewing reports

Open **Reports** from the sidebar to see your report history. Each report includes:

- **Overall period score** (1–10)
- **Category scores** — training load, recovery, progress, consistency
- **Written analysis** — AI narrative with specific references to your workouts
- **Recommendations** — actionable focus areas for the next period

Click a report to read the full analysis. Reports can be exported for sharing with a coach.

## When reports generate

Reports are created:

- **Automatically** on a schedule (weekly/monthly, depending on your activity level)
- **On demand** via Chat — _"Generate my weekly report"_

Background generation runs asynchronously. You receive a notification when a report is ready.

## Report scores

Each category is scored 1–10 with an explanation:

- **Training Load** — was volume and intensity appropriate for your phase?
- **Recovery** — did you balance stress with adequate rest?
- **Progress** — are fitness markers moving in the right direction?
- **Consistency** — did you complete planned sessions?

See [Metrics & Scoring](/documentation/athletes/metrics-scoring) for how scores relate to daily workout analysis.

## Sharing with your coach

If you work with a human coach on Coach Watts, they can review the performance, recovery, schedule, and workout information available in their permission-checked [coach view](/documentation/coaches/acting-as-athlete). You can also share report links directly.
