title: Activities & Workout Analysis
description: View synced workouts, understand AI analysis scores, and manage your training calendar in Coach Watts.
---

The **Activities** page is your training calendar and workout history. Every synced session gets AI analysis with scores that explain how well you executed relative to your plan and physiology.

## Activities calendar

The calendar view shows:

- **Completed workouts** — synced from Strava, Garmin, Intervals.icu, and other sources
- **Planned workouts** — from your [training plan](/documentation/athletes/training-plans)
- **Rest days** and events

Click any day to see sessions. Use filters to show specific sports or date ranges. Calendar view also supports rescheduling planned workouts, matching completed workouts to planned sessions, scheduling library templates, and merging duplicate completed activities.

See [Activity Calendar & Workout Management](/documentation/athletes/activity-management) for step-by-step instructions.

## Workout detail page

Opening a workout reveals:

- **Summary** — duration, distance, TSS, average and normalized power/pace
- **Intervals** — lap and segment breakdown with zone distribution
- **Charts** — power, heart rate, pace, elevation streams
- **AI analysis** — scores and written feedback
- **Map** — GPS route (when available)

## AI analysis scores

After each workout, the AI generates **1–10 scores** across multiple dimensions:

| Score         | What it measures                             |
| ------------- | -------------------------------------------- |
| **Overall**   | Holistic session quality                     |
| **Technical** | Power/pace consistency, cadence, form        |
| **Effort**    | How hard the session felt relative to target |
| **Pacing**    | Evenness of effort distribution              |
| **Execution** | Adherence to prescribed intervals and zones  |

Click any score for the **"Why"** breakdown and actionable improvements.

See [Metrics & Scoring](/documentation/athletes/metrics-scoring) for the full scoring framework.

## Planned workouts

Planned sessions live at **Workouts → Planned** or on the calendar. Each planned workout includes:

- Structured intervals (warmup, main set, cooldown)
- Target zones and expected TSS
- Link to matched completed activity

### Workout matching

When a synced activity corresponds to a planned session, Coach Watts links them automatically. Unmatched pairs can be linked manually from the plan dashboard.

## Uploading workouts

Manually upload `.fit` files via **Dashboard → Upload** or **Workouts → Upload**. Useful for:

- Indoor trainer sessions without auto-sync
- Older files not in connected platforms
- Third-party device exports

## Sharing workouts

Share a workout analysis with a coach or friend:

1. Open the workout
2. Click **Share**
3. Copy the public link

Shared links show summary stats and analysis — review what is included before sharing.

## Duplicate activities

If the same workout syncs from multiple sources (e.g., Garmin and Strava), Coach Watts can identify the records as versions of one activity.

- Configure future automatic processing under **Settings → Apps → Ingestion Settings**.
- Run a reviewed history scan from **Activities → Deduplicate**.
- Drag one completed workout onto another to merge a specific pair.
- Open a workout's **Versions** section to promote or unlink a source record.

Secondary versions remain accessible; they are not silently discarded. See [Duplicate Workouts & Versions](/documentation/athletes/duplicate-workouts) for detection, primary selection, merge behavior, and corrections.
