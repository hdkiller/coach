A structured workout is a planned session with intervals or strength blocks—not just a title and duration. This page covers the full lifecycle for endurance structures; for gym-specific blocks see [Strength Training](/documentation/athletes/strength-training).

## Lifecycle overview

1. **Create** a template in **Library → Workouts** (or save from Chat / a completed session).
2. **Schedule** it onto **Activities** (or let a plan / coach place it).
3. **Export** to your device or virtual platform when needed.
4. **Complete** the session outdoors or indoors.
5. **Sync**, **match** to the plan, and **compare** adherence.

## Create a template

### From the library

1. Open **Library → Workouts**.
2. Select **New Workout** and choose the sport (Ride, Run, Swim, Gym, and so on).
3. Build intervals or strength blocks in the editor: targets (power, HR, pace), durations, repeats, and coach notes.
4. Save and optionally file in a folder.

![Workout library grid of reusable structured sessions with sport filters and folder organization](/media/docs/library-workouts.webp)

### From Chat or AI generation

Ask Chat to generate a structured session, review the preview, and approve when prompted. Generation uses your sport profiles and quotas — see [AI Chat & Supported Tools](/documentation/athletes/chat-interaction) and [AI Coach Settings](/documentation/athletes/settings/ai-coach).

### From a completed workout

On a completed workout detail page, use **Save to Library**, then edit the template so it becomes a prescription rather than a recording of one day.

## Schedule

1. Open **Activities** → calendar view.
2. Open the workout drawer, pick the template, and place it on a date.
3. Confirm **Schedule Workout**.
4. Open the planned workout to verify zones and targets before training day.

Keep thresholds current under **Settings → Sports** so targets resolve correctly — [Sport Profiles, Thresholds & Zones](/documentation/athletes/settings/sport-profiles).

![Activities calendar used to place and track planned versus completed structured sessions](/media/docs/activities-calendar.webp)

## Export to devices and apps

On a **planned** workout, open **Download Workout** (wording may vary) when available:

| Format              | Typical use                         |
| ------------------- | ----------------------------------- |
| **Zwift (`.zwo`)**  | Import into Zwift workout library   |
| **Garmin (`.fit`)** | Load onto a Garmin device / Connect |

Some APIs also support additional trainer formats; the UI exposes the formats available for that workout. After exporting, follow the destination app's import steps.

Connected platforms such as [ROUVY](/documentation/athletes/integrations/rouvy) or [Intervals.icu](/documentation/athletes/integrations/intervals-icu) may also publish or sync structures when those integrations are enabled — see each integration guide.

## Complete and sync

Train with the structure on your device or virtual platform, then:

1. Let the activity sync from [connected apps](/documentation/athletes/integrations), or upload a `.fit` file from the Dashboard.
2. On **Activities**, match the completed file to the planned workout (drag or **Link Workouts**).
3. Open the completed detail for AI analysis, plan adherence, intervals, and map.

If the same ride appears twice, resolve duplicates before trusting scores — [Duplicate Workouts & Versions](/documentation/athletes/duplicate-workouts).

## Compare planned vs completed

- **Plan adherence** on the completed workout shows how execution tracked the prescription. Regenerate after changing the link or structure.
- **Workout Comparison** — add multiple sessions and open comparison for metrics, intervals, or streams. See [Analytics Explorer & Workout Comparison](/documentation/athletes/analytics-tools).

## Troubleshooting

### Targets look wrong on the device

Update FTP / pace / HR thresholds, re-open the planned workout, and export again. Percent-of-threshold steps follow your current sport profile.

### Structure is empty or missing steps

Wait for background generation to finish (Chat or plan generation can run asynchronously). Open the planned workout and regenerate structure if the UI offers it. Confirm you are not looking at a description-only shell.

### Completed file will not match

Check start time and sport, unlink a wrong match, then use **Link Workouts**. Very short or manually created activities may need a manual link.
