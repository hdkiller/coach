The Coach Watts AI is not just a chatbot—it's a deeply integrated training assistant with access to your real-time data, plans, and history.

## What the Coach Can Do

You can interact with your coach via the **Chat** tab. The coach has access to specialized tools to help you manage your life as an athlete.

### 1. Manage Your Workouts

You can ask the coach to create, update, or find workouts:

- _"Schedule a 90-minute zone 2 ride for tomorrow morning."_
- _"I'm feeling tired, can we make today's intervals easier?"_
- _"Show me my last 3 running sessions."_

### 2. Analyze Your Progress

Ask for deep dives into your performance:

- _"How is my aerobic fitness trending over the last month?"_
- _"Compare my power output from this morning to my last 2x20 threshold session."_
- _"Why is my recovery score so low this week?"_

### 3. Nutritional Guidance

Get advice based on your metabolic data and training load:

- _"What should my carb intake look like for my 4-hour ride on Saturday?"_
- _"Give me a high-protein meal plan for a double-workout day."_

## How the Chat Works

### Durable Conversations

Every message you send is part of a **Durable Turn**. This means:

- **Persistence**: Your conversation is saved and can be resumed across devices.
- **Background Execution**: If you ask for a complex training block, the AI works in the background and will notify you when it's done.
- **Real-time Updates**: When the coach modifies your plan, the changes appear instantly on your Dashboard and Calendar.

### Best Practices for Interaction

- **Be Specific**: Instead of "Make me a plan," try "Generate a 4-week base-building block for a 100km cycling event in June."
- **Provide Context**: Mention how you feel. _"I slept poorly last night, should I still do my HIIT session?"_ allows the coach to factor in your recovery data.
- **Trust the Tools**: When the coach triggers a tool (like `create_planned_workout`), you will see a preview of the action. You can always ask it to refine the result if it's not perfect.
