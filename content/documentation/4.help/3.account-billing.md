This page covers subscription management for athletes and coaches. Platform billing uses the same **Settings → Billing** surface for both roles when payments are enabled for the deployment.

## Plans

Coach Watts offers three tiers:

| Plan          | Best for                                    | Highlights                                           |
| ------------- | ------------------------------------------- | ---------------------------------------------------- |
| **Free**      | Getting started and manual workflows        | Manual sync, on-demand analysis, limited AI usage    |
| **Supporter** | Self-coached athletes who want automation   | Automatic sync, always-on analysis, higher AI quotas |
| **Pro**       | Adaptive planning and proactive AI coaching | Deep AI engine, proactive insights, highest quotas   |

Current list pricing appears on **Settings → Billing** and the public [pricing](/pricing) page. Annual billing usually costs less than paying monthly.

## Open Billing

1. Go to **Settings → Billing**.
2. Review your active plan, renewal date, and entitlement summary.
3. Use the actions on that page to upgrade, change plan, open the payment portal, or sync status.

If Billing is missing from Settings, payments are not configured for this deployment (common in self-hosted setups). Feature access then follows the deployment defaults.

## Upgrade or change plan

1. Open **Settings → Billing**.
2. Choose **Free**, **Supporter**, or **Pro** and a monthly or annual interval when offered.
3. Complete checkout.
4. Wait for Coach Watts to confirm activation before starting another checkout or refreshing repeatedly.

Paid subscribers can open the subscription portal (**Manage** / **Manage via Stripe**) to update the payment method, switch plans, download invoices, or cancel.

## Cancel or stop renewal

1. Open **Settings → Billing**.
2. Select **Manage** to open the Stripe customer portal.
3. Cancel auto-renewal or the subscription there.

Access typically continues through the end of the paid period shown on the Billing page. Canceling checkout before payment leaves your current plan unchanged.

## Past-due payments

A past-due banner means the payment provider could not charge your method.

1. Open the billing portal from **Settings → Billing**.
2. Update the card or retry payment.
3. Return to Coach Watts and wait for status to refresh (use **Sync** if shown).

Some features may stay restricted until the subscription returns to an active state.

## Entitlements vs AI quotas

- **Entitlements** control capability (for example automatic sync, always-on analysis, proactive AI). See the **Your Entitlements** section on Billing.
- **AI quotas** limit how often you can run chat, analysis, plan generation, and related operations. Open **Settings → AI Coach** for remaining usage and reset windows.

Upgrading can raise quotas and unlock entitlements. It does not repair a failed provider import or missing workout file.

## Account export and deletion

Billing cancellation does not delete your account.

- **Export data** — **Settings → Danger Zone** (also summarized in [Data & Privacy](/documentation/help/data-privacy))
- **Delete account** — **Settings → Danger Zone** (permanent)

## Troubleshooting

### I paid but still see Free

Wait a minute for webhook confirmation, then use **Sync** on Billing. Avoid opening multiple checkout sessions. If it stays wrong, open a ticket in the [Help Center](/help-center).

### I canceled in the App Store / Play Store

Platform subscriptions purchased through an app store must be managed in that store's subscription settings. Use **Settings → Billing** for web/Stripe subscriptions.

### Coaches and athlete fees

Coach Watts platform billing covers your Coach Watts subscription. Collecting coaching fees from athletes is handled outside Coach Watts (for example your own invoicing or the external CTA on a public Start page).
