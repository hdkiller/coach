---
title: Coaching Calendar
description: Review athlete schedules, compare two calendars, and schedule, move, copy, inspect, or delete planned workouts.
---

Open **Coaching → Calendar** to manage training without leaving the coach workspace.

## Select athletes and a view

The left roster rail lets you search athletes and choose a **Primary** athlete. Turn on **Split** and select **Compare** to place a second athlete beside the first.

In split view:

- **Synced** navigation moves both calendars through time together.
- **Unlocked** navigation lets each calendar use a different date.
- **Swap** exchanges the primary and comparison lanes.

Choose **Week board** for day-by-day planning or **Month grid** for a broader schedule. Use the arrow controls or **Today** to navigate.

## Understand calendar items

- Amber items are planned workouts.
- Red planned items are missed.
- Green items are completed activities.
- Blue items indicate a planned/completed match.
- A readiness badge shows the day's recovery score when available.

The day summary shows planned and completed counts. Dense days may collapse extra items behind a “more” count.

## Schedule from the workout library

1. Open the **Workouts** rail or drawer.
2. Find a template in the selected library scope or folder.
3. Drag it to an athlete's day, use a quick schedule target, or open the calendar picker.
4. Confirm the athlete and date.

## Schedule from a plan blueprint

Open **Plans**, search or filter templates, then open a plan. Expand its blocks and weeks. You can drag an individual plan workout to a day or use its plus menu to schedule it to an available athlete/date target.

The calendar does not apply the whole blueprint in this flow; it schedules the individual workout you choose. Use **Open in Library** when you need the full plan overview.

## Move, copy, inspect, and delete

- Drag a planned workout to another day to move it.
- In split view, drag a planned workout into the other athlete's lane to copy it to that athlete and day. The original remains on the source athlete's calendar.
- Select a planned workout to open its details. Coach calendar mode hides athlete completion and structure-generation actions, but allows appropriate edits and deletion.
- Select a completed workout to open a coach-facing summary with duration, TSS, average heart rate, and CTL/ATL.

## Compare completed workouts

Hover a completed activity and select **Add to comparison**. The workout comparison dock collects the selected sessions and opens the analytics comparison workflow.
