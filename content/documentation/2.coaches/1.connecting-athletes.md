---
title: Connecting with Athletes
description: Connect athletes by share link, email invitation, personal code, or public coaching request.
---

Open **Coaching → Athletes** and select **Invite Athlete**. Coach Watts supports several onboarding paths so you can use the right one for an existing user, a specific prospect, or a wider community.

## Choose a connection method

| Method                | Best for                                           | What you do                                                  |
| --------------------- | -------------------------------------------------- | ------------------------------------------------------------ |
| **Share Link**        | Communities, social posts, or an open cohort       | Generate one link or QR code and share it                    |
| **Invite by Email**   | A specific athlete                                 | Enter their email; Coach Watts emails a restricted join link |
| **Connect by Code**   | An existing athlete who gave you their code        | Enter the athlete-generated personal coach code              |
| **Public Start Page** | Prospects who should answer intake questions first | Review and approve or decline their coaching request         |

Coach-generated share and email invitations expire after **seven days**. Create a new invitation if the old one expires.

## Create a share link

1. Go to **Coaching → Athletes**.
2. Select **Invite Athlete**, then **Share Link**.
3. Select **Create Share Link**.
4. Copy the URL, copy the code, or open the QR-code view.
5. Share it with prospective athletes.

The link is not restricted to one email address. Revoke it from **Pending Invitations** when you no longer want it to be usable.

## Invite one athlete by email

1. Select **Invite Athlete → Invite by Email**.
2. Enter the athlete's email address.
3. Select **Send Invitation**.

Only the invited email address can accept a restricted invitation. If email delivery fails, the invitation is automatically revoked rather than left active.

## Connect with an athlete's code

Use **Connect by Code** when an existing athlete has generated a personal **Invite a Coach** code:

1. Ask the athlete for their active code.
2. Enter it in **Invite Athlete → Connect by Code**.
3. Select **Connect Athlete**.

This creates the direct coach–athlete relationship immediately when the code is valid.

## After an athlete joins

The athlete appears in **My Athletes**, the strategic overview, and the coach calendar. Open their card to review coach-visible performance and schedule information.

Athletes remain in control of direct coach access and can remove a coach from **Coaching → Team → My Coaches**.
