To begin coaching on the Coach Watts platform, you must establish a secure connection with your athletes. This ensures data privacy and explicit consent from the athlete.

## How Connections Work

Coach Watts uses a **secure invite code system**. Athletes generate a unique, time-sensitive code that they share with their coach. Once you enter this code, you gain access to their performance data and the ability to manage their training plan.

## Steps for Coaches

1. **Get the Code**: Ask your athlete to provide their **Coach Invite Code**.
2. **Open Coaching Dashboard**: Navigate to the [Coaching](/coaching) page in your sidebar.
3. **Add Athlete**: Click the **"Add Athlete"** button in the top right.
4. **Enter Code**: Paste the 6-character code (e.g., `AB12XY`) provided by your athlete.
5. **Start Coaching**: Once connected, the athlete will appear in your "My Athletes" list.

## Steps for Athletes

If your athlete is unsure how to find their code, you can guide them with these steps:

1. Navigate to the **Coaching** page.
2. Select the **"My Coaches"** tab.
3. Click **"Generate Invite Code"**.
4. Copy the code and send it to your coach.

> [!NOTE]
> Invite codes are valid for a limited time. If a code expires before you use it, the athlete will need to generate a new one.

## Managing Connections

- **Removing an Athlete**: Coaches can currently remove athletes from their dashboard by contacting support.
- **Removing a Coach**: Athletes can revoke a coach's access at any time from the "My Coaches" tab by clicking **"Remove"**.
