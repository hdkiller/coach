Coach Watts separates direct invitations from public coaching requests. Both can create a coaching relationship, but they begin differently.

## Pending invitations

**Coaching → Athletes → Pending Invitations** lists active coach-generated invitations. Each entry shows its code, optional restricted email, and expiry.

- A **share link** can be accepted by any eligible athlete who has the link.
- An **email invitation** is restricted to the invited email.
- Both expire after seven days.
- Use the revoke action to invalidate an invitation immediately.

Creating **New Invite** or **New Share Link** does not extend the existing invitation; it creates a new one with its own code and expiry.

## Public coaching requests

When your [Coach Start Page](/documentation/coaches/start-direct-invite-pages) intake form is enabled, submitted requests appear above the roster.

Each request can include:

- the athlete's identity and submission time;
- their answers to your custom intake questions;
- a notice listing active coaches they already work with.

Select **Approve** to create the coaching relationship or **Decline** to reject the request. Review the athlete's answers and existing-coach notice before approving.

## Team invitations

Team invites are managed inside a team rather than on the main athlete invitation list. Owners and admins can create staff or athlete codes, restrict them to an email, choose a role, and optionally auto-assign athletes to a group.

Staff can also generate a reusable athlete team link or QR code from the team roster.

## Troubleshooting

- **Expired code** — generate a new invitation.
- **Wrong email** — revoke the restricted invite and send a new one to the correct address.
- **Athlete already connected** — use the existing roster entry instead of creating another relationship.
- **Team code does not work** — confirm the invite is still pending, unexpired, and intended for the signed-in email if restricted.
