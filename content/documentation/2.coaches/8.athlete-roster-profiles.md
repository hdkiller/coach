Open **Coaching → Athletes** for your full direct-coaching roster. Search and group tools help you organize larger squads, while each athlete card summarizes current status.

## Athlete cards

A connected athlete card can show fitness, fatigue, form, compliance, next planned workout, next event, and alerts for high fatigue or low compliance. Select **Analyze Athlete** to open the detailed coach view.

Masked cards show **Private Profile** and cannot be opened. This can happen in team contexts where roster visibility is broader than private-data access.

## Overview tab

The athlete overview combines:

- current CTL, ATL, and TSB;
- readiness and the latest readiness date;
- seven-day planned/completed compliance;
- a 30-day fitness and fatigue trend;
- sleep score, HRV, resting heart rate, and weight when available;
- upcoming training and recent completed work;
- the latest recommendation status.

Missing values appear as `--`. A missing value is not a zero and usually means the athlete has not synced or recorded enough relevant data.

## Calendar tab

Use this tab for a concise schedule pulse:

- planned and completed counts for the last seven days;
- overdue planned workouts;
- upcoming events;
- upcoming planned sessions;
- recent completed sessions marked as **Matched** or **Ad hoc**.

Select **Open Coach Calendar** for scheduling and calendar actions.

## Zones tab

The zones tab shows the athlete's FTP, lactate-threshold heart rate, maximum heart rate, and configured power and heart-rate zones. Use the values as context when reviewing workout targets; the athlete controls their underlying sport profile settings.

## Completed workout summary

Selecting a recent completed session loads a coach-scoped summary. The coach calendar also allows completed workouts to be added to Workout Compare.
