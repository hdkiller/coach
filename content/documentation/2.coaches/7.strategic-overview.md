title: Strategic Overview
description: Use weekly compliance, recent athlete activity, and roster signals on the coach dashboard.
---

**Coaching → Overview** is the operational starting point for a coach. It combines this week's planned-versus-completed work with a recent activity feed and roster shortcuts.

## Weekly compliance

Each athlete row shows the current week as status markers:

- completed and unscheduled-completed sessions;
- partially completed work;
- missed sessions;
- planned sessions;
- days without a session.

Expand a row for more detail or use **Profile** to open that athlete's coach view. The small markers on roster cards summarize the latest five compliance states.

## Recent athlete activity

The activity feed surfaces recent completed work across connected athletes. Use it to spot new sessions before opening the athlete profile or calendar for detail.

## Roster signals

The overview displays up to eight roster cards as quick links. Select **Manage Roster** for invitations, groups, requests, and the full athlete list.

## Quick actions

The top bar links directly to:

- **Add Athlete** — opens the athlete roster and onboarding area;
- **Workouts** — reusable workout templates;
- **Exercises** — the strength exercise library;
- **Plans** — plan blueprints.

## A practical review rhythm

1. Scan the week for missed, partial, or overloaded patterns.
2. Check the recent feed for sessions that explain the change.
3. Open the athlete profile for recovery and fitness context.
4. Use the coach calendar to adjust upcoming work.
5. Use Analytics when the decision needs a longer time window or comparison.
