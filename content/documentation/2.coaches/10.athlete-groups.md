---
title: Athlete Groups
description: Organize direct and team rosters into functional groups without changing coaching relationships.
---

Groups are roster filters for squads such as “Junior Team,” “Race Build,” or “Injury Return.” They do not create or remove coach–athlete relationships.

## Create a group

1. Go to **Coaching → Athletes** or a team's **Groups** tab.
2. Select **Manage Groups**.
3. Select **Create New Group**.
4. Enter a name and optional description.
5. Choose team scope when you want the group visible to other coaches in that team.

## Add and remove members

Open a group in **Manage Groups**, select an available athlete, and add them. Existing members are listed in the same dialog and can be removed from the group.

Removing someone from a group only changes organization. It does not remove them from your direct roster or from a professional team.

## Personal and team scope

- A personal group organizes your own coached-athlete view.
- A team-scoped group is visible to the team's staff.
- Team athlete invitations can auto-assign new members to a selected team group.

## Filter the roster

Select a group chip to show only its athletes. Select **All** to restore the complete roster. An empty filtered view means no current roster athletes belong to that group.

## Delete a group

Delete a group from the group manager when it is no longer needed. This removes the grouping, not the athletes, coaching relationships, workouts, or team.
