The Library stores reusable training content. The main sections are **Workouts**, **Exercises**, and **Plans**.

## Library content

| Section       | Use it for                                                                     |
| ------------- | ------------------------------------------------------------------------------ |
| **Workouts**  | Structured workout templates, targets, intervals, and reusable session designs |
| **Exercises** | Strength and mobility exercise definitions used in strength sessions           |
| **Plans**     | Multi-block, multi-week training blueprints and their workouts                 |

Workout and plan libraries support folders, search, bulk movement, and item previews. Plans also support favorites and publication settings.

## Create and organize content

1. Open the appropriate Library section.
2. Create a workout, exercise, or plan.
3. Set the sport, structure, targets, and descriptive fields supported by that editor.
4. Save it and place it in a folder if needed.

Plan blueprints can be edited in the Plan Architect, where you manage blocks, weeks, workouts, public details, and publication options.

## Plan visibility

Open a plan's **Share settings** to choose:

- **Private** — visible only to the owner and an assigned coach where applicable.
- **Team** — visible to all coaches and athletes in the selected team.
- **Public** — listed in the public plan marketplace after its public details are configured.

Sharing controls who can discover and use a blueprint; it does not silently add its workouts to every athlete's calendar.

## Use library content with athletes

The most direct coach workflow is **Coaching → Calendar**:

- use the **Workouts** drawer to schedule a workout template for a chosen athlete and date;
- use the **Plans** drawer to browse coach, athlete, team, favorite, or public content and schedule an individual workout from a plan.

When a whole plan's **Use Plan** dialog is available in coaching context, choose the target athlete, start date, and whether to replace incomplete future workouts from that date.

## Coach and athlete ownership

Some library interfaces show **Coach**, **Athlete**, or **Both** sources while coaching context is active. The owner badge matters when editing or deleting content: access to an athlete's calendar does not automatically transfer ownership of their templates.
