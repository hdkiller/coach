---
title: Applying & Updating Plans
description: Apply plan blueprints to athletes, update live schedules, and manage Private, Team, and Public share settings.
---

A **plan in the Library** is a reusable blueprint. An athlete's **Training Plan** (or the dated workouts on their calendar) is the live schedule created from that blueprint or from AI generation. This page covers coach workflows for applying and maintaining blueprints.

## Plan visibility

Open a plan's **Share settings**:

| Visibility  | Who can discover / use it                                      |
| ----------- | -------------------------------------------------------------- |
| **Private** | Owner and an assigned coach where applicable                   |
| **Team**    | Coaches and athletes in the selected professional team         |
| **Public**  | Listed in the public plan catalog after public details are set |

Sharing controls discovery. It does **not** automatically place every workout on every athlete's calendar.

![Plan library with reusable blueprints, folders, and share-ready templates](/media/docs/library-plans.webp)

## Apply a whole plan to an athlete

When the coaching **Use Plan** dialog is available:

1. Open the plan from **Library → Plans** or from the calendar **Plans** drawer.
2. Choose **Use Plan** (wording may vary).
3. Select the **target athlete**, **start date**, and whether to **replace incomplete future workouts** from that date.
4. Confirm and review the athlete calendar for the first week.

If only individual workouts are available from a blueprint, schedule them one at a time from **Coaching → Calendar** — that places sessions without activating the full blueprint lifecycle. See [Coaching Calendar](/documentation/coaches/coaching-calendar).

![Coach calendar used to place plan workouts onto an athlete schedule](/media/docs/coaching-calendar.webp)

## Schedule without applying the whole plan

From **Coaching → Calendar**:

1. Open the **Plans** drawer.
2. Browse coach, athlete, team, favorite, or public content.
3. Schedule a single workout from the blueprint onto a date.

Use this for one-off sessions or when you do not want to replace the athlete's existing forward schedule.

## Update a live schedule

After workouts are on the calendar:

- **Move / copy / delete** sessions on **Coaching → Calendar**.
- Edit the **library blueprint** in Plan Architect when you want future applications to change — editing the blueprint does not silently rewrite every athlete who already applied an older copy unless you re-apply or manually adjust.
- Prefer clear communication with the athlete before replacing incomplete future workouts.

## Unshare or change visibility

1. Open the plan → **Share settings**.
2. Switch from Public/Team back to Private, or revoke team scope.

Athletes who already copied or applied content keep the dated workouts already on their calendar. Unsharing stops new discovery; it is not a remote wipe of athlete calendars.

## Public catalog notes

Public plans appear in the shared catalog for discovery. Publishing is not the same as charging for a plan inside Coach Watts—commerce, if any, happens through your own process or external CTA on your Start page.

## Ownership

Coach and athlete libraries are separate. Access to an athlete calendar does not transfer ownership of their templates. Use the source selector (**Coach** / **Athlete** / **Both**) when coaching context is active. See [Coach Library & Sharing](/documentation/coaches/coach-library).
