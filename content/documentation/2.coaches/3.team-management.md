title: Team Management
description: Create or join a professional team, manage roles, invite staff and athletes, and organize the roster.
---

Professional teams let multiple staff members work in one organization with a shared roster and team-scoped groups.

## Create or join a team

Go to **Coaching → Team**:

- Select **Create Team**, enter a name and optional description, then confirm.
- Select **Join Team**, enter a team invitation code, then confirm.

Your team cards show your role plus member and group counts. Select a card to open the team workspace.

## Roles and visible tabs

| Role        | Team access                                                       |
| ----------- | ----------------------------------------------------------------- |
| **Owner**   | Roster, groups, staff, management, invitations, and team deletion |
| **Admin**   | Roster, groups, staff, management, and invitations                |
| **Coach**   | Roster, groups, staff, and athlete onboarding                     |
| **Athlete** | Team roster and staff; private athlete details can be masked      |

Only owners and admins see the **Management** tab. Only the owner can delete the team.

## Add athletes

On the team **Roster** tab, staff can select **Add Athlete** and choose one of three paths:

1. **Share Invite** — generate a link or QR code, optionally auto-assigning new athletes to a group.
2. **From My Athletes** — add someone who already has a direct coaching relationship with you.
3. **By Invite Code** — enter the athlete's personal coach code; this connects them to you and adds them to the team.

## Invite staff or a specific athlete

Owners and admins can open **Management → Create Invite** and configure:

- an optional email restriction;
- the **Athlete**, **Coach**, or **Admin** role;
- an optional auto-assignment group for athlete invitations.

Team invitations expire after seven days. Active invitations show their role, email restriction, group, and expiry. Use the trash action to revoke one.

## Team groups

Staff can create team-scoped groups from the **Groups** tab. Other team coaches can see these groups. Use them to filter the roster and to auto-organize athletes joining through a team link.

Group membership and team membership are different: removing an athlete from a group does not remove them from the team.

## Delete a team

The owner can use **Management → Danger Zone → Delete Team**. This permanently removes the team and its groups. The interface asks for confirmation and the action cannot be undone.
