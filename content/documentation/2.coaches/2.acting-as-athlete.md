---
title: Acting as an Athlete
description: Learn how to view the platform from your athlete's perspective.
---

One of the most powerful features for human coaches is the ability to **"Act As"** an athlete. This allows you to see exactly what the athlete sees on their dashboard, review their AI recommendations, and adjust their training plans.

## How to "Act As" an Athlete

1. Navigate to the **Coaching** page.
2. Ensure you are on the **"My Athletes"** tab.
3. Locate the athlete you wish to monitor.
4. Click the **"View"** or **"Manage"** button on their athlete card.

### What Happens Next?

- You will be automatically redirected to the **Dashboard**.
- A notification banner will appear at the top of the screen indicating which athlete you are currently managing.
- **Full Visibility**: You can now navigate through the athlete's Activities, Nutrition, and Training Plans as if you were logged into their account.
- **Actionable Insights**: You can review the AI-generated "Today's Recommendation" for that athlete and provide your own manual adjustments.

## Stopping the Session

To return to your own coach account:

1. Look for the **Impersonation/Acting-As Banner** at the top of the screen.
2. Click the **"Stop Acting As"** or **"Return to Coach Profile"** button.
3. You will be returned to your coaching dashboard.

> [!TIP]
> Use this feature during scheduled check-ins to review recent workout data together with the athlete, ensuring your manual coaching strategy aligns with the platform's AI insights.
