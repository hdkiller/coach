# Release v0.5.24

Hey Coach Watts community! 👋

We've been hard at work behind the scenes, and we're thrilled to unveil our latest update, v0.5.24! This release is packed with incredible new features and improvements designed to supercharge your training and coaching experience. Get ready for a smoother, more powerful, and more intuitive Coach Watts! ✨

Here's what's new:

## 🚀 Game-Changing New Features!

### 🗺️ The All-New Plan Architect & Training Plans

We've completely overhauled how you create, manage, and share training plans!

- **Build & Apply Plans with Ease:** Design comprehensive plans and apply them directly to your calendar! 🗓️
- **Public Plan Library & Profiles:** Create a public profile and share your training plans with the world, or discover awesome plans from other coaches! 🌐
- **Advanced Plan Organization:** Keep your plans tidy with folders and mark your favorites for quick access. 📁❤️
- **Intuitive Drag & Drop:** Effortlessly move workouts from your library directly into your training plans. ✨
- **Enhanced Plan Insights:** Get a clearer picture with new plan overviews, detailed weekly breakdowns, activity-specific charts, seasonal load timelines, and even mini workout charts! 📊
- **Notes & Templates:** Add notes to your plans and utilize new templates for quicker plan creation. 📝

### 👑 Powerful Coaching & Team Management

Coaches, we've got some big wins for you!

- **New Coaching Dashboard:** A central hub to view your athletes' calendars, manage your team, and track compliance. 🚀
- **Shareable Team Invites:** Easily invite athletes to your team with a simple, shareable link! 🤝
- **"Act As User" Persistence:** When coaching, switch to an athlete's view and stay there across the app for seamless management. 🧘

### 🧠 Smarter AI Assistant

Your AI assistant is getting even more intelligent!

- **AI Memory Management:** You can now manage what your AI assistant remembers about you, allowing for more personalized and effective recommendations. 💡
- **Improved Unit Handling:** The AI will now consistently respect your preferred units (imperial/metric) in its prompts and responses.

### 🔬 Deeper Analytics & Insights

Unlock new levels of understanding for your performance.

- **Workout Comparison Tool:** Dive deep into your workouts! Compare up to three activities side-by-side on a dedicated page, directly from your calendar, or within activity details. See how you stack up! 🆚
- **Dashboard Renaming:** Personalize your dashboards and widgets by giving them custom names.
- **Nutrition Presets & Unit Formatting:** Better tools for tracking and analyzing your nutrition data. 🍎
- **Adherence Analysis:** New sport-specific settings for more accurate adherence analysis.

### 🏋️ Streamlined Workout Management

Managing your workouts just got a whole lot easier.

- **Visual Intensity Manipulation:** Adjust workout intensity with intuitive visual controls. 💪
- **Save to Library & Quick Scheduling:** Easily save workouts to your library and quickly schedule them from the workout drawer or activities page.
- **Workout Folders:** Organize your workout templates into folders for better management. 🗂️
- **Resizable Workout Drawer:** Customize the workout drawer size to fit your needs.

## 🌟 Quality of Life Improvements & Refinements

- **Mobile-Friendly Layouts:** We've optimized many pages, including the Library and Plan Architect, for a better experience on your phone and tablet. 📱
- **Refined UI & Styling:** A fresh look and feel across the app with updated colors and components for a more modern and consistent experience. ✨
- **Improved Calendar Performance:** Your calendar will load faster and feel snappier! ⚡
- **Better Timezone Handling:** More accurate activity recommendations with improved timezone logic.
- **Wellness Overlays:** Toggle to include daily check-ins in your wellness charts for a complete picture.
- **Dashboard & Nutrition Updates:** Enhanced calendar data handling for a smoother experience on these pages.
- **Unified Logout:** A more consistent and secure logout experience.

## 🛠️ Key Fixes

- **Workout Chart Fixes:** Resolved issues in workout run charts for accurate data display.
- **Drag & Drop Reliability:** Fixed minor glitches for a smoother drag-and-drop experience in the Plan Architect.

We're incredibly excited for you to explore all these enhancements and elevate your Coach Watts experience. Thank you for being a part of our journey!
